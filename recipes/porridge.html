Porridge

Description

Good old breakfast dish

Ingridients

  • Water
  • Oats
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Measure 1 part of oats, 2 parts of water and pour it into the casserole
  2. Keep it on medium heat until it starts boiling
  3. After your porridge begin boiling low your heat and add salt and sugar by taste, keep it around 5 minutes
  4. Close casserole with lid and keep it 5 minutes before serving
