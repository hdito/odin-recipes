Grilled Cheese

Description

It's a grilled cheese. With two loaves of bread.

Ingridients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Drop the butter in the pan on medium heat
  2. Wait til it starts sizzle
  3. Drop bread in the pan and flip it after it become gold
  4. Put slice of cheese on your bread and flip it on your pan
  5. After cheese has melted crush it with another toast and serve
