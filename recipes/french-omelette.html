French omelette

Description

Delicious tender breakfast

Ingridients

  • Eggs
  • Parsley
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs with salt and chopped parsley
  2. Pour them in pan on low heat
  3. Constantly stir them after little fibres start to create
  4. After there are a little bit of uncooked egg let omelette grip roll it and serve
